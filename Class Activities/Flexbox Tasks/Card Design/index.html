CINEMA

CINEMA is the debut studio album of American indie pop band The Marías, released June 25th, 2021. Its capricious development began even before The Marías had formed. It was their only album to feature bassist Ariel Ferman, as she was fired from the band four months after its release for refusing to get a COVID-19 Vaccine Test due to Abdominal compartment syndrome, and was also their only album as a five-piece.
